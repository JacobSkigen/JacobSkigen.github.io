Research Experience
======
### Graduate Student Researcher (Jan 2021 - Present) ###
_Orthotics & Prosthetics for Enhanced Mobility Laboratory, Newark, DE, USA_  
Supervisor: Dr. Elisa Arch, PhD
* Quantifying the interaction between the post-stroke lower limb system and passive-dynamic ankle-foot orthoses using a combination of joint-level energetics and electromyography
* Took over and completed a multi-year study to assess the benefits of passive-dynamic ankle foot orthoses with personalized stiffnesses for individuals post-stroke
* Wrote two manuscripts for publication in high-impact scientific journals using data from my prior project, with more in progress
* Mentored junior lab members and undergraduates in lab equipment setup, data collection, and data processing
* Contacted, recruited, and scheduled study participants  

### Undergraduate Student Researcher (Sep 2017 - May 2020) ###
_Neuromuscular Rehabilitation Engineering Laboratory, Raleign, NC, USA_  
Supervisor: Dr. Helen (He) Huang, PhD
* Aided with the design and construction of a novel perturbation platform for testing postural control in lower-limb amputees
* Ran a pilot study using the novel setup to determine whether it elicits anticipatory postural adjustments in subjects
* Designed and executed a pilot study to quantify differences in lower limb performance while crossing a variety of obstacles using motion capture and kinematic data
